## Diagnoses worth remembering

- The `getsentry/warden` composite action inherits PATH Node — needs `setup-mise` (Node 22+) or its
  bundled undici crashes on `webidl.util.markAsUncloneable`.
- `WARDEN_MODEL` must be in the Pi runtime's _bundled_ model catalog, not just valid on OpenRouter;
  an uncataloged slug fails every chunk with a misleading "usually an authentication problem" error.
  The two failure classes are distinguishable: catalog miss reports `all_hunks_failed`, a real auth
  failure reports `provider_unavailable`.
