# blog-content

Begin writing blog posts for djf.io. Distinct from `blog-style-improvement` (visual/UX polish) —
this is the words. David sets the ideas and voice; Claude helps outline, draft, and edit.

## Workflow

- Posts live in `apps/djf.io/src/content/blog/` (MDX; authored with semantic line breaks —
  djf.io's `src/content/` is excluded from Prettier rewrapping).
- David provides the angle/thesis; Claude drafts and edits in David's voice; David reviews on the
  live preview (per-PR preview deploys now exist).
