## 2026-07-01

### fix(f311x): bump wrangler to 4.105.0 to satisfy vite-plugin peer dependency

Lockfile maintenance advanced `@cloudflare/vite-plugin` to 1.42.3, whose `wrangler` peer dependency is `^4.105.0`, but the app still pinned `wrangler` at 4.103.0 -- so `vite build` failed CI with "The installed version of Wrangler does not satisfy the peer dependency required by @cloudflare/vite-plugin (^4.105.0)". Pinned `wrangler` to 4.105.0 (the minimum satisfying version) and regenerated the lockfile.
